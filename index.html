Snake Plants

The (almost) unkillable houseplant.

The perfect houseplant:

If you are longing for a house plant but fear the burden of taking care of it, look no further then the snake plant. It it an easy-going, almost unkillable plant. It is from the Sansevieria family and sometimes goes by the name 'mother-in-law's tongue'. There is a huge varitey and comes in many shape, sizes and patterns.

5 Reasons to have a Snake plant:

  • 1 - They are (almost) unkillable.
  • 2 - They are suitable for most light conditions and thrive when they are left alone.
  • 3 - Does not require lots of watering. Snake plants enjoy a light watering and can survive in dry conditions. Great for the forgetful minds!
  • 4 - Provides air purification. The snake plant stores oxygen throughout the day and releases it during the night. Perfect for bedrooms!
  • 5 - There are more than 70 different species - you've bound to find one you like!
Top tip: It's best to underwater the plant than to overwater it.
